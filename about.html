Team Xeystroke

We set out to provide a quick and simple set of musical tools for musicians. Our tools are designed to by used in the studio or on-set with minimal setup allowing you freedom to play.

A quick glance under Chords allows you find chords in the key desired. Pulling up the Notes page gives a fast reference to the notes in your key.Our Collaborate page... does something?

This project will keep us building and making it better with new keys and awesome ways to share your creations. If there is some feature you don't see and want it please let us know by submitting your idea below.
